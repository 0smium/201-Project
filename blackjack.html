HOW TO  

How To Play

  • The goal of the game is to score higher than the dealer, but less than or equal to 21.
  • You go first. You can 'hit' (draw) as many times as you want to try to reach 21.
  • If you go over 21, you 'bust' and the dealer automatically wins.
  • When you think you've reached your highest, safe score, 'stay' to end your turn and give the dealer a try.

How To Bet

  • Your can only change your bet between rounds.
  • Your bet cannot exceed your money on hand.
  • If you bet higher, the game will correct your bet to whatever you have left.
  • If you win, you double your bet.
  • If you lose, you also lose your bet.
  • If you tie, you get your bet back.
  • You can 'Double Down' to double your bet. Only do this if your initial hand looks good (9-11 points). You will be dealt one card. The above rules apply.
